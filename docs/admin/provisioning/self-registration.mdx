## Setting Up Self Registration

This guide explains how to enable and configure self-registration for users on your Passbolt instance.

### Admin Setup

**Step 1.** Log in with an administrator account and go to the administration panel.

<Figure
  src={'/img/help/2025/04/Admin1.png'}
  caption="fig. Navigate to admin tab"
  alt="Admin tab"
/>

**Step 2.** Enable the Self Registration feature and specify allowed email domains.

Only users with an email address from the specified domains will be able to self-register.

:::warning
Avoid using free or public email domains (e.g., gmail.com) to prevent unauthorized registrations.
:::

<Figure
  src={'/img/help/2025/04/Admin4.png'}
  caption="fig. Enter allowed domains"
  alt="Enter domains"
/>

**Step 3.** Save your settings.

Once done, eligible users can initiate the registration process themselves via your Passbolt URL.
